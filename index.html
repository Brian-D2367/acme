LIST OF DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

OWNERSHIP

  • Lease deed & Society Registration, Tenement Details updated in CDCO records. Private Survey of Plot & Risks.
  • Proof of Building Age above 30 years or proof of building or Dilapidated condition Certificate C1 / Structural Audit Report.
  • Development Program (DA) / Power of attorney.
  • Registration of Association/from Registrar of Co-operative Societies.
  • Authority Letter given to Office Beavers of Association in Original.
  • Consent Letter for Amatganation / Subdivision / Reconstruction from members of Association in original.
  • List of membership/internematerials certified from CDCOLID.
  • Plot Chain of documents, Demarcation plan, Plan, tenement Piers etc. from CDCOLID.
  • DP Remark from NMMC TPO.
  • Redevelopment proceeds as per 79 (A).

NOCs

  • CDCO NOC for Demotion, Premium, TDR & Ancillary FSI
  • Height NOC from Airport Authority of India.
  • NOC from Property Tax / MERCL / MGL / Water / Tiger / FRE / MCE Department.
  • NOC from Financial Institute when flat is mortgaged.
  • Title Clearance Certificate from Advocate.
  • Any other NOC as required by CDCO / NMMC from time to time.
  • NOC from CDCO, in case of sharing of FSI is proposed.
  • NOC from City Engineering Section for Road Widening, off any!
  • NOC from CDCO Ltd. for Redevelopment.
